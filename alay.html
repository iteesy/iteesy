My poetic journey started here.

Alay [ah-lie] (Tagalog for "offering") is a print/art organization housed under Student Media/Bayanihan at the University of California, Santa Cruz. For over 20 years, Alay annually publishes an anthology that contains art submissions (photography, poetry, drawings, etc.) as a collective medium of self-expression from students of color. I took on the role as one of the editor-in-chiefs in 2018.

The theme was Sunrise / Sunset to represent the day in the lives of BIPOC and the Filipino youth community through art.
