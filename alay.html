My journey to poetics started here.

Alay [ah-lie] (Tagalog for "offering") is a print/art organization housed under Student Media/Bayanihan at the University of California, Santa Cruz. For over two decades, Alay has released a yearly anthology showcasing artistic submissions including photography, poetry, and drawings. This collection serves as a collaborative platform for self-expression from students of color, exploring how culture influences their diasporic identity. One year, I took on the role as one of the editor-in-chief.

The theme was Sunrise / Sunset to represent the day in the lives of BIPOC and the Filipino youth community through art.
