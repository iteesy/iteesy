about

a Designer through Raconteur

User Experience Designer at VMware living the quaintness of San Francisco, CA.

I was born and raised up and down California. Growing up, my best friend was the internet which led me to study Cognitive Science with a concentration in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of California, Santa Cruz.

I’m constantly in the pursuit of investigating the potential relationships we have with our interfaces. That we are evoked by collections of rectangles and text with connections that can be identified by our intuition.

You can say that I’m forever indebted to my first desktop, my first laptop, and every other hand-me-down device.
